The AuBackup mobile app offers a backup feature that helps send your full resolution images and videos to your PC via either Wi-Fi or USB or both. The whole backup process is comprised of three steps:
1. **PC Setup**: Install the AuBackup PC client by downloading the dmg package from the official website - https://aurora.boldman.net . Launch it and click the "Add Folder" button to add a backup folder on your PC and then follow the on-screen instructions to show a QR code.
2. **Mobile Setup**: Open the AuBackup mobile app and click the "Scan QR Code" button to scan the QR code displayed on your PC. AuSearch and AuBackup will establish a trust relationship before starting the data transfer securely.
3. **Backup Process**: Grant the necessary permissions on your mobile device and select the media files you want to back up. The app will then transfer the selected files or all your photo library to the designated folder on your PC. You can plug in your mobile device via USB any time during the transfer to speed up the transfer process.
