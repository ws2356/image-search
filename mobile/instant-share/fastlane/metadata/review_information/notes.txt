Steps to test this app:

1. Install the SnapGet desktop app from https://www.boldman.net/snapget.html#download
2. Enable the SnapGet share extension on macOS: System Settings > General > Login Items & Extensions > Extensions > Sharing
3a. (PC to mobile) Ensure PC and iPhone are on the same Wi-Fi. Right-click files on macOS and share with the SnapGet share extension, which shows a QR code. Scan the QR code with the SnapGet in-app camera or system camera app.
3b. (Mobile to PC) Ensure PC and iPhone are on the same Wi-Fi. Share an image or browser link from iPhone, select SnapGet, then select the discovered PC and follow the UX flow to complete sharing.
