Share photos and text from your iPhone to your computer instantly.

AuShare adds a Share Extension to your iOS device so you can send content directly to your PC from any app. No cloud, no cables, no extra steps.

How it works:
1. Open AuSearch on your computer (the companion desktop app).
2. In any app, tap the Share button and choose AuShare.
3. Select your computer from the discovered devices.
4. Confirm the PIN on both devices to establish trust.
5. Your content is transferred instantly over your local Wi-Fi network.

Why people use AuShare:
- Send photos from any app in two taps
- Share text snippets directly to your desktop clipboard
- End-to-end encrypted transfers over your local network
- No cloud upload required
- Works over Wi-Fi with no cables
- One-time trust setup with PIN confirmation
- Companion desktop app (AuSearch) is free

Built for people who want a fast, private way to move content from their phone to their computer.
