AuBackup moves photos and videos from your iPhone to your PC without routing your library through the cloud.

Pair in seconds by scanning a QR code from AuSearch on your desktop, then transfer full-quality items over Wi-Fi or switch to USB for even faster backups. Reconnect to the same desktop later and keep your library moving with a simple, privacy-friendly workflow.

Why AuBackup:
• Direct iPhone-to-PC backup
• Full-quality photo and video transfer
• Fast QR pairing with your desktop
• Works over Wi-Fi and USB
• Local transfer flow designed for privacy
• Easy reconnects to previously paired desktops

Start on your PC, add a Mobile Folder in AuSearch, scan the QR code, and back up your library in minutes.
