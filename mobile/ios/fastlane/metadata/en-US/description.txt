Back up full-quality photos and videos to your computer with no cloud upload required.

AuBackup pairs with AuSearch on your desktop in seconds using a QR code, then keeps your library local over Wi-Fi or USB and ready for natural-language search on desktop.

Why people use AuBackup:
- Move photos and videos directly to your computer
- Keep full quality and metadata intact
- Pair quickly with a desktop QR code
- Use Wi-Fi for convenience or USB for faster transfers
- Keep your library off third-party cloud storage
- Search your backed-up library using natural language in AuSearch on desktop

How it works:
1. Open AuSearch on your computer and add a Mobile Folder.
2. Scan the QR code in AuBackup.
3. Choose Wi-Fi or USB and start backing up your library.
